Accessibility Statement

Last updated: May 2026

Beit Building Contractors LLC is committed to making our website accessible to people of all abilities, including those who use assistive technologies.

Standards

We aim to meet the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, Level AA, and to comply with the Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA).

What We've Done

  • Semantic HTML structure with proper headings and landmarks.
  • Descriptive alt text on every meaningful image.
  • Keyboard navigation support throughout the site.
  • Visible focus styles on interactive elements.
  • Color contrast tested against WCAG AA standards.
  • Reduced-motion support for users who prefer less animation.
  • Skip-to-main-content link at the top of every page.

Known Limitations

Some background videos play automatically without sound. They are decorative and labeled with aria-hidden so screen readers skip them.

Feedback

If you encounter an accessibility barrier or need information in an alternative format, please contact us. We respond to accessibility requests within 5 business days and will make reasonable accommodations.
