Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Beit Building Contractors LLC ("we", "us", or "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect through this website, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information We Collect

  • Contact information you provide: name, email, phone number, property location, and any details you include when requesting a quote or contacting us.
  • Standard server data: IP address, browser type, referring page, and pages viewed, used solely to operate the site.

How We Use Information

  • To respond to your estimate request or other inquiry.
  • To schedule and complete the work you request.
  • To follow up about your project, warranty matters, or future services if you opt in.
  • To comply with applicable laws and protect our legal rights.

How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share it only with:

  • Email-delivery services we use to route your estimate request to our office inbox and to send you the receipt of your request. Current providers include Resend, Web3Forms, and the underlying email infrastructure they rely on.
  • Hosting and database providers (Vercel and Upstash) used to store your request for up to 90 days while we follow up; the record is automatically deleted at the end of that window.
  • Optional team-notification services we may configure (Slack, Discord) so the team sees new requests in real time. These are opt-in per environment and are subject to the same retention rules.
  • Subcontractors and suppliers we engage to complete your project, only as needed.
  • Government, insurance, or legal authorities when required by law.

Storage and Retention

Estimate requests are stored for a maximum of 90 days, after which they are automatically purged from our database. We never store your IP address; it is used only at the network edge for rate-limiting purposes (to protect the form from spam and abuse) and is never written to any persistent log.

Cookies

This site does not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics. We do not load Google Analytics, the Meta Pixel, or any other cross-site tracking tools. Embedded fonts are loaded from Google Fonts, which may set its own cookies — see Google's policies for details.

Your Choices

You can request that we delete the contact information you submitted, or opt out of follow-up communications, by emailing beitbuilding@gmail.com or calling (407) 942-6459.

Children

This site is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect their information.

Florida Residents

If you are a Florida resident, you have the right to know what personal information we have about you and to request its deletion. Contact us using the information above to exercise these rights.

Updates

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will always be posted on this page.
